Privacy Policy

This extension does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data from users.

All operations are performed locally in the user's browser. No external servers or tracking tools are involved.

  • Location data
  • Financial or health data
  • Authentication information
  • Browsing history or online activity

This extension operates with a single purpose: providing watermarking functionality directly in your browser.
